Service 01

AC Repair in Durango

A malfunctioning air conditioner in Durango's heat is more than an inconvenience — it's urgent. Our certified technicians diagnose and repair all makes and models, quickly and accurately.

Common Problems We Fix

  • AC blowing warm or hot air
  • Unit not turning on or frequently cycling off
  • Unusual noises (grinding, banging, squealing)
  • Refrigerant leaks and low coolant levels
  • Frozen evaporator or condenser coils
  • Thermostat malfunctions and sensor issues
  • Water leaks or excessive condensation
  • Electrical failures and tripped breakers

What You Get With Our Repair Service

  • Thorough diagnosis to identify the real root cause
  • Clear, upfront repair estimate before we start
  • Quality parts and professional-grade repairs
  • Work guaranteed — if it fails again, we return at no charge
  • Friendly technicians who explain what they're doing

Why professional repair matters: A DIY fix or an unqualified technician can turn a minor issue into a compressor replacement — the most expensive repair in HVAC. Our certified team gets it right the first time and extends the life of your system.

Service 02

AC Installation in Durango

Installing an air conditioning system is one of the biggest comfort investments you'll make for your home or business. Done right, it delivers years of efficient, trouble-free cooling. Done wrong, it means constant repairs and high electricity bills.

Is It Time for a New Installation?

  • New construction home or commercial space with no existing AC
  • Adding air conditioning to a room or area without cooling
  • Converting from fans or evaporative coolers to central or split AC
  • Expanding an existing system to cover additional areas

Our Installation Advantage

  • Free in-home consultation and load calculation for proper sizing
  • Expert recommendation of the right system for your space and budget
  • Clean, professional installation following all manufacturer specs
  • Full system testing and walkthrough before we leave
  • Warranty registration and after-install support

Why proper sizing is critical: An undersized unit struggles and fails early. An oversized one short-cycles, wastes energy, and doesn't properly dehumidify. Our technicians perform a proper load calculation to ensure your new system is perfect for your space.

Service 03

AC Replacement in Durango

Is your old air conditioner costing you more to run than a new one would? Are repairs piling up year after year? It may be time to replace your system — and when you do, Guenther Aire y Confort makes the process seamless and stress-free.

Signs You Need AC Replacement

  • Your system is 10–15+ years old
  • Repair costs are approaching 50% of a new unit's price
  • Electricity bills have risen significantly without explanation
  • Your system uses R-22 refrigerant (now phased out)
  • Inconsistent temperatures or constant breakdowns
  • Excessive noise, odors, or poor air quality

Benefits of Replacing Your Old System

  • Lower electricity bills with modern energy-efficient units
  • Quieter, more consistent cooling throughout your space
  • Improved air quality and humidity control
  • No more unexpected repair bills
  • Full manufacturer warranty on your new system
  • Environmentally friendly refrigerants

Our honest approach: We'll never push you toward a replacement if a repair makes more sense. We give you the facts, the numbers, and an honest recommendation — and you make the call.

Service 04

Preventative AC Maintenance in Durango

The best way to avoid a breakdown in the middle of summer is to never let it happen in the first place. Our preventative maintenance program keeps your system running at peak performance all year long.

What Happens Without Regular Maintenance?

  • Efficiency drops — electricity costs climb
  • Dirty coils and filters force the system to overwork
  • Small issues go unnoticed until they become costly failures
  • System lifespan is significantly shortened
  • Warranty may be voided without documented maintenance

What's Included in Our Tune-Up

  • Full system inspection and performance evaluation
  • Cleaning of condenser and evaporator coils
  • Filter check and replacement (if needed)
  • Refrigerant level check and top-off if required
  • Electrical connections inspection and tightening
  • Thermostat calibration and testing
  • Drainage system cleaning and inspection
  • Written report of your system's condition

Prevention pays: Regular maintenance typically costs a fraction of an emergency repair and can extend your system's lifespan by 5–10 years. Ask about our annual maintenance plan for priority scheduling and discounted service.
